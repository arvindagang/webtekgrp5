Vision Statement

To become a fully developed and danger free community managed by God-loving, just and selfless leaders through taking changes, succeeding in difficult situations and promoting service excellence for self-reliance citizenry living in a peaceful and an environment friendly community.

Mission Statement

To provide fair assistance, embrace integrity as the foundation of public trust, facilitate harmonious relationships among the constituents and enhance access to information and materials that will contribute positively to the community in the attainment of goals and protection of lives and properties.
